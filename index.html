About Vagabond Studios

A passion project born from our love of urban exploration
& the stunning beauty of historic architecture.
Though we've spent many stints abroad & on the move in years past, 
currently our studio is based in the Ravenswood neighborhood of Chicago.

Every print is carefully illustrated to accentuate the ornamental details of these architectural treasures.
We especially love the craftsmanship and attention to detail
common in the era at the turn of the twentieth century.
